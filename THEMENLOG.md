## 2026-04-17T14:30:00+02:00 | DESIGN_PRINCIPLE | Progressive Disclosure

Rather than designing down to the worst user, the best UIs use progressive disclosure — simple by default, powerful on demand. This respects both novice and expert users by:

* Showing the basics first
* Revealing advanced options contextually or on request
* Never punishing expertise while still protecting beginners

Identified during reflection on Module 4 design strategy. Relevant to multi-lens analysis feature (auto-mode vs expert-mode), Criterion Operationalizability Protocol UI, and general interface philosophy across aXIOM modules.
